About Me

Hey there, I'm Shruti! I'm an Electrical Engineer with a passion for building smart, tiny things — from sensors and microsystems to innovative product designs. I'm deeply interested in how things are made, from precision manufacturing and microfabrication to scaling up ideas into real products. Whether it's designing in CAD, refining a manufacturing process, or experimenting with new materials, I love bringing ideas to life. I'm always curious, always learning, and excited to take on new challenges. When I'm not engineering, you’ll find me traveling, cooking, baking, or dreaming up the next big innovation.
